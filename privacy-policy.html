Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Welcome to Easy Quran (“we”, “our”, or “us”). Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Easy Quran mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

Basic use of Easy Quran does not require an account. However, you have the optional choice to sign in via your Google Account.

If you choose to sign in, we collect your basic profile information (such as email and name) to sync your data across devices. Additionally, if you contact us for support, we may collect the information you voluntarily share.

b. Automatically Collected Information

The app may collect limited, non-personal data to improve performance, such as:

  • Device model and OS version
  • App version and crash logs
  • Anonymous usage analytics (if enabled)

This data helps us enhance app stability and user experience.

c. App Permissions

Easy Quran may request the following permissions to provide specific features:

  • Location Permission: Used to calculate and display accurate prayer times (Salat) based on your current city or region. This location data may be processed in the background to ensure accurate prayer times and timely notifications.
  • Notification Permission: Used to send you timely alerts for Namaz/prayer times and alarms you set within the app.

d. No Other Sensitive Data

We do not collect or store any other sensitive personal data, such as biometric or financial information.

2. How We Use Information

Collected information (if any) is used solely to:

  • Provide accurate prayer times and notifications based on your location
  • Improve the app’s design, functionality, and performance
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Respond to user requests and feedback

We never sell, rent, or share your information for advertising or tracking.

3. Data Storage and Syncing

  • All Quran text, translations, and tafseer data are stored locally on your device.
  • If you choose the optional Google Sign-in, your data—including goal progress, collections, bookmarks, and app preferences—is securely synced to the cloud to allow access across devices.
  • If you do not sign in, your preferences and bookmarks remain only on your device.
  • We take reasonable measures to keep your data secure and private.

4. Third-Party Services

To ensure quality and performance, Easy Quran may use trusted third-party tools:

  • Google Play Services – app updates & delivery
  • Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics – crash reports & anonymous performance metrics

These services operate under their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy

5. Children’s Privacy

Easy Quran is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The app contains no ads, no tracking, and no in-app purchases.

6. Links to External Sources

The app may include references or links to external Quran repositories or resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites.

7. Consent

By using Easy Quran, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue using the app.

How can we help?

If you are experiencing any technical issues, have questions about features, or want to provide feedback on Easy Quran, please don't hesitate to reach out. Our mission is to make Quran learning accessible and seamless for everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is the app free? Yes, Easy Quran is completely free with no ads or in-app purchases.
  • Does it work offline? Yes, once you download the translations or mushaf pages, they are available offline.
  • How do I sync my progress? You can sign in with your Google account to sync your goals and bookmarks across devices.

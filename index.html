Frequently Asked Questions

Is Easy Quran app free?

Yes, Easy Quran is completely free to download and use with all features included.

Can I use Easy Quran offline?

Yes, after initial download, all content is available offline without internet connection.

Which translations are available?

English, Urdu, Arabic, Bengali, Russian, Azerbaijani, and 11+ global languages are available—with tafseer insights to deepen understanding.

Is Easy Quran app proofread or reviewed by scholars?

Yes. The Arabic Quran text of Easy Quran is reviewed and authenticated by qualified Islamic scholars. A verified Shariah Board Certificate (in English and Urdu) is also attached above on this page.

Is Easy Quran ad-free?

Yes. Easy Quran is completely ad-free. There are no banners, video ads, or pop-ups — ensuring a distraction-free and peaceful Quran reading experience.

Which scholar's tafseer does Easy Quran include?

Easy Quran provides tafseer from renowned scholars including Ibn Kathir, Maududi, and others — allowing you to compare explanations and understand verses deeply.

Can I create personal collections of verses?

Yes. You can save any verse and organize it into your own custom collections — such as patience, charity, motivation, family values, reminders, or spiritual reflections.

Does Easy Quran support topic-based browsing?

Yes. Easy Quran allows you to explore the Quran by topics such as charity, family values, justice, finance, patience, gratitude, social ethics, and many more.

Is Easy Quran the only app that classifies Ayahs by topics?

Yes. Easy Quran is currently the only Quran app that automatically organizes verses into meaningful topics such as charity, patience, gratitude, justice, family values, finance, and social ethics.
